Overview

Enjoy an award winning dinner cruise with your family, friends. Enjoy an International buffet created and served by the Radisson Blu. You will have assigned private allocated seating for your group, either on the open air upper deck or lower restaurant area depending on your preference (please state you preference upon booking and may be pre-selected on availability) . Once in position enjoy a fresh juice and relax to the live acoustic from the in-house musician as the cruise glides you through the night lights of Dubai. The buffet will open shortly after departure where you will feast on international cuisine, including a Salad bar with local inspiration and a mix of international with 1 live cooking station and an array of sweets to tickle anyone's fancy. What To Expect

Choose a sunset or later evening cruise on booking, and travel independently to the departure pier at Dubai Marina. Step out along the red carpet onto the prettily lit, glass houseboat. With an upper, open-air deck and lower, glass-enclosed deck—the plush, spacious boat is perfectly designed for relaxing over dinner and soaking in the views. Sip a welcome drink, and admire the skyscrapers around the marina, either as their lights switch on at sunset, or they’re fully illuminated against the night sky. Then, enjoy the pleasant breezes as your boat sails along the marina, passing landmarks and then take in the stunning panoramas of the new Bluewaters Island, and city skyline before turning back. Along the way, savor a delicious buffet dinner of international dishes alongside sodas, juices, water, tea, and coffee. See the Itinerary for a sample menu. As you eat, enjoy gentle background music and a live guitarist, and after dinner, relax outdoors to relish the views again before returning to the pier.
